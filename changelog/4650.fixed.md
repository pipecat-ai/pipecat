- Fixed `LiveKitTransport` leaking audio/video stream readers when a track is unsubscribed: the owned `rtc.AudioStream`/`rtc.VideoStream` and its producer task are now closed and cancelled on unsubscribe (and on a re-subscribe for the same participant), so a client republishing its mic (e.g. mute/unmute or text↔voice toggles) no longer accumulates concurrent producers that interleave audio into the shared queue and silence downstream STT.
