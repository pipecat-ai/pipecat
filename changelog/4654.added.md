- Direct functions advertised in an `LLMContext` are now registered automatically — no separate registration call. List a direct function in `LLMContext(tools=[...])`, or push an `LLMSetToolsFrame` to change tools mid-session, and its handler is registered. The advertised tool set is the single source of truth: dropping a direct function unregisters its handler too. Also applies across `LLMSwitcher` member LLMs.
