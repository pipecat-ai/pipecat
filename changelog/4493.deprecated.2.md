- `PipelineTask`, `PipelineTaskParams`, and the `pipecat.pipeline.task` module have been renamed to `PipelineWorker`, `WorkerParams`, and `pipecat.pipeline.worker`. The old names still resolve (the module re-exports the new symbols) but constructing `PipelineTask` / `PipelineTaskParams` emits a `DeprecationWarning`; they will be removed in a future release.
