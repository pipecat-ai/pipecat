- `SpeechTimeoutUserTurnStopStrategy`, `TurnAnalyzerUserTurnStopStrategy`, and `ExternalUserTurnStopStrategy` now accept a `wait_for_transcript: bool = True` kwarg. When flipped to `False`, the strategy signals end-of-turn as soon as its requirements are met, minus waiting for transcripts — useful when you intend to configure local turn detection to drive realtime service conversations, where waiting for transcripts is unnecessary latency. `LLMContextAggregatorPair` flips this for you when `realtime_service_mode=True`.
