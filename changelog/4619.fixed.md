- Fixed multi-worker bus broadcast contamination in `_BusEdgeProcessor`. In a setup with multiple bridged `PipelineWorker`s, emitter-local frames (`TTSSpeakFrame`, `FunctionCallsStartedFrame`, `FunctionCallInProgressFrame`, `FunctionCallResultFrame`, `FunctionCallCancelFrame`, `MetricsFrame`) were delivered through the bus to every sibling worker, causing duplicate context entries in the assistant aggregator, double TTS synthesis, and inflated metrics counters. The receive-side filter now drops these frames at sibling edges; the main `PipelineWorker`'s `BusBridgeProcessor` (which legitimately consumes them — e.g. routing a sub-bot's `TTSSpeakFrame` to its hosted `TTSService`) is unaffected.
