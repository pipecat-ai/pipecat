- `PipelineRunner` has been renamed to `WorkerRunner` and moved to `pipecat.workers.runner`, since the runner now runs workers (of which `PipelineWorker` is one kind), not just pipelines. Import `WorkerRunner` from `pipecat.workers.runner`. The old `pipecat.pipeline.runner` module still re-exports both names, and `PipelineRunner` still works as a subclass alias, but it emits a `DeprecationWarning` and will be removed in a future release.
