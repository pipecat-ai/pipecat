- Fixed `LLMService` silently dropping an `LLMUpdateSettingsFrame` whose `system_instruction` happens to equal the current composed `_settings.system_instruction` (e.g. after `append_system_instruction`). The refresh of `_base_system_instruction` and the recompose are now gated on the delta declaring `system_instruction`, not on `Settings.apply_update`'s diff against the (possibly composed) `_settings` value.
