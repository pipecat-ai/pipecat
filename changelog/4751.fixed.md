- Fixed an issue in `AggregatedFrameSequencer` where delayed word-timestamps from
  an interrupted (cleared) TTS context could be emitted as passthrough
  `TTSTextFrame`s with `append_to_context=True`, interleaving stale words into the
  next turn's transcript (observed with Inworld TTS in `ASYNC` mode). Words for an
  unknown or cleared context are now dropped instead of corrupting the active turn.
