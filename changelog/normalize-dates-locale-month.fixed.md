- Fixed `normalize_dates` (the date voice formatter used by `VoiceFormatter(normalize_dates=True)`) rendering the month name via `strftime("%B")`, which honors the process `LC_TIME` locale. On a host with a non-English locale (e.g. `de_DE`, `fr_FR`, `es_ES`) this produced a mixed-language spoken date such as "Mai 10th, two thousand and twenty-three" alongside the hardcoded-English year and ordinal suffix. Month names are now always English.
