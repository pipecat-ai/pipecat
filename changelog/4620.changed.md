- `AzureSTTService` now marks final transcripts as finalized. Azure's `RecognizedSpeech` event is by definition the final recognition for an utterance, so the emitted `TranscriptionFrame` carries `finalized=True`. This lets downstream user-turn stop strategies (e.g. `SpeechTimeoutUserTurnStop`) take their finalized fast-path instead of waiting for VAD events that may never arrive on short replies.
