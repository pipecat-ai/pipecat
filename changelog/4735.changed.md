- `pipecat create` now infers `--bot-type` from the chosen transports in non-interactive mode, so the flag is optional: a bot is `telephony` when any transport is a telephony transport (twilio, telnyx, plivo, exotel, daily_pstn, twilio_daily_sip) and `web` otherwise. Pass `--bot-type` explicitly to override (it's still validated and cross-checked against the transports); the interactive wizard is unchanged.
