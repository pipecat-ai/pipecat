- Fixed `TaskManager.cancel_task()` swallowing the caller's own cancellation. If the calling task was itself cancelled while suspended awaiting the child task, the resulting `CancelledError` was caught by the generic `except CancelledError: pass` and the caller resumed as if it had never been cancelled. Service teardown paths that await `cancel_task()` from a `finally` block (e.g. `DeepgramSTTService._connection_handler` cancelling its keepalive task) could then survive the pipeline's `CancelFrame`, iterate their reconnect loop, and reconnect after having been cancelled — leaving an orphaned reconnect loop running forever.
