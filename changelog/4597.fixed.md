- Fixed the start of user speech being clipped from transcripts when `GeminiLiveLLMService` is configured for locally-driven turns (server-side VAD disabled). The problem was that any audio sent up to Gemini Live before sending `activity_start` (sent when user turn start is confirmed) seemed to get discarded; the service now replays (sends to Gemini Live) a short audio "pre-roll" right after `activity_start`, so the onset is preserved.
