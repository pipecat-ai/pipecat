- Fixed a benign `ERROR` log line emitted by `UltravoxRealtimeLLMService` during client-driven teardown. When the client hangs up, the Ultravox websocket's close handshake can race the receive loop and raise `ConnectionClosedError` from outside the existing `try/except`, bypassing the `_disconnecting` guard that should have silenced it. Hoisting the `try` around the `async for` correctly silences this.
