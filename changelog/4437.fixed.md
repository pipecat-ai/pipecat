- Fixed `WhatsAppWebhookRequest` parsing to tolerate Meta terminate-error payloads, which routinely omit `href` and `error_data` and use `title` instead of `message`. `WhatsAppError` keeps `code` required (always present in observed payloads) and adds an explicit `title` field; backwards-compatible with payloads carrying the full shape. Resolves a `ValidationError` flood on calls that fail with codes such as 138021 ("no media for a long time").
