- Fixed `expand_currency` (used by `VoiceFormatter`) leaking the 3rd and later fractional digits of a currency amount onto the subunit word. `$5.500` expanded to `five dollars and fifty cents0` and `$3.567` to `three dollars and fifty-six cents7`, so TTS spoke the stray digit. The full fraction is now consumed and read to cent precision (e.g. `five dollars and fifty cents`), dropping sub-cent precision like a two-digit amount.
