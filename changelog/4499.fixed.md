- Fixed ElevenLabsTTSService re-emitting `voice_settings` on every audio-context init. ElevenLabs' multi-context WebSocket protocol rejects subsequent context-init messages that include `voice_settings` with WS close 1008 (`voice_settings field must be provided in the first message and then either be not provided or not change`). After PR #4433 made each turn open a fresh context, the resend fired on every turn after the first and silenced the bot mid-response. `voice_settings` is now sent only on the first context init after a fresh WebSocket connection and re-sent on reconnect.
