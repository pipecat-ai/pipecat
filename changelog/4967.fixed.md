- Fixed `TurnAnalyzerUserTurnStopStrategy` keeping stale speech state across externally-ended user turns. When a turn ended by any path other than the analyzer's own COMPLETE (a forced or external stop, or the stop watchdog timeout) while a mute strategy held audio back, the smart turn analyzer stayed frozen mid-speech and its `stop_secs` silence timer later fired a phantom end-of-turn during the bot's response (spurious `on_user_turn_inference_triggered` / `on_user_turn_stopped`, and with realtime services like Gemini Live a stray `activity_end` that could drop the user's next utterance). Stop strategies now get a `handle_user_turn_stopped()` callback, called by `UserTurnController` on every turn end (immediately before `reset()`) and never at turn start, and the turn-analyzer strategy clears its analyzer there. Start strategies get a symmetric `handle_user_turn_started()` callback for consistency. The `pre_speech_ms` buffer refills continuously from incoming audio, so the next turn regains its full pre-speech context within `pre_speech_ms` of the clear; only a user restarting within that window (about half a second) sees one prediction with a shortened lead-in.
