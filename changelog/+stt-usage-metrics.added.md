- Added STT usage metrics: `DeepgramSTTService` and `SonioxSTTService` report usage as `STTUsageMetricsData`, carrying the client-measured seconds of audio submitted to the service (`audio_seconds`), emitted incrementally per final transcript with a flush on stop/cancel. Enabled with `enable_usage_metrics=True`; usage is forwarded to RTVI clients (`stt_usage`), logged by `MetricsLogObserver`, and attached to OpenTelemetry `stt` spans as `metrics.audio_seconds`.
