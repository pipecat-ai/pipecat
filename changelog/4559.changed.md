- ⚠️ RTVI protocol version bumped to `2.0.0`. The `bot-output` message now includes `will_be_spoken`, `spoken_status` (`"new"` / `"in-progress"` / `"completed"`), `spoken_progress` (accumulated/remaining text), and `segment_id` fields. Clients on protocol `1.4.x` are still served with the legacy format but receive a deprecation warning; all other pre-2.x clients are rejected.
