- Fixed the output transport silently dropping `TTSTextFrame`s for already-spoken words on interruption. `MediaSender.handle_interruptions()` destroyed its pacing queue outright, so text for audio that had already been sent to the caller never reached the assistant context aggregator, causing the bot to lose track of what it had said. Already-queued `TTSTextFrame`s are now flushed before the queue is reset or replaced.
