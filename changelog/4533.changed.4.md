- `SpeechTimeoutUserTurnStopStrategy`, `TurnAnalyzerUserTurnStopStrategy`, and `ExternalUserTurnStopStrategy` now accept a `wait_for_transcript: bool = True` kwarg. When set to `False`, the strategy signals end-of-turn as soon as VAD / the turn analyzer / external `UserStoppedSpeakingFrame` reports end-of-speech rather than waiting for a transcript — useful when local turn detection is the intended driver of a realtime conversation, or when an external service is the authoritative turn signal. `LLMContextAggregatorPair` flips this for you when `realtime_service_mode=True`.
