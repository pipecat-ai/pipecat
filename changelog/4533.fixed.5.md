- `InworldRealtimeLLMService` and `GrokRealtimeLLMService` no longer broadcast `UserStartedSpeakingFrame`/`UserStoppedSpeakingFrame` when configured for manual (locally-driven) turn detection. Both services' server-side speech-started/stopped events fire in manual mode too, but in that setup turn frames are expected to come from local turn detection (e.g. a `vad_analyzer` in `LLMUserAggregatorParams`) — without the gate, the services were broadcasting alongside the locally-emitted frames, producing duplicate `on_user_turn_*` events. OpenAI Realtime was already correct here: its server doesn't fire speech events in manual mode at all.
