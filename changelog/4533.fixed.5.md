- `InworldRealtimeLLMService` no longer broadcasts `UserStartedSpeakingFrame`/`UserStoppedSpeakingFrame` when configured for manual (locally-driven) turn detection. Inworld's server-side speech-started/stopped events fire in manual mode too, but in that setup turn frames are expected to come from local turn detection (e.g. a `vad_analyzer` in `LLMUserAggregatorParams`) — without the gate, the service was broadcasting alongside the locally-emitted frames, producing duplicate `on_user_turn_*` events. OpenAI Realtime and Grok Realtime were already correct here: their servers don't fire speech events in manual mode at all.
