- Deprecated `LLMService.register_direct_function()`, `LLMService.unregister_direct_function()`, and `LLMSwitcher.register_direct_function()`. Direct functions are now registered automatically: list them in `LLMContext(tools=[...])` for tools available at session start, or push an `LLMSetToolsFrame` to change tools mid-session (both register, and unregister, handlers automatically). These methods will be removed in a future version.
