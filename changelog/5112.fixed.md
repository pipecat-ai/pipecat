- Fixed `SmallWebRTCTransport` silently dropping app messages sent before the peer connection was established — e.g. the RTVI `user-mute-started` emitted by `MuteUntilFirstBotCompleteUserMuteStrategy` at pipeline start, which left clients that connect slower than the pipeline starts (real networks; loopback usually wins the race) never showing the greeting-hold mute. Messages sent before the data channel is open are now buffered and delivered, in order, once it opens; messages sent while the connection is closing are discarded with a debug log.
