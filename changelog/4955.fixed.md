- Fixed `BaseWorker.send_job_stream_end` not removing the finished job from `_active_jobs`. Streaming jobs stayed "active" after the stream ended, so a cancel or update that raced in afterwards was still processed (firing `on_job_cancelled` and sending a `CANCELLED` response for an already-completed job) and the job leaked until worker shutdown. It now clears the job like `send_job_response` does.
