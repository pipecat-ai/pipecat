- Switched `BaseSmartTurn` from `time.time()` to `time.monotonic()` for its three internal interval-math sites (audio-buffer timestamps, speech-start tracking, and the pre-speech buffer-trim loop). Wall-clock time can step forward or backward when NTP adjusts the system clock, which would silently corrupt the buffer trim (prune everything / prune nothing) and the speech-window extraction. The corrected primitive is monotonic and matches the existing `time.perf_counter()` usage already in place for inference-latency metrics.
